About Me

Welcome! Thanks for taking the time to review my portfolio. I am a recent career changer who is working to break in to the tech industry. After graduating college, I worked in various marketing and communications roles. I enjoyed the fast-paced environment and the collaboration behind the creation of creative ways to promote new initiatives. While both challenging and fun, I consistently found myself wanting to engage with my interests in technical and creative problem-solving. Therefore, I decided to use my unique experience and education, to put these skills to work and change careers. As an aspiring full-stack developer, I have had a lot of fun learning so many new things since taking the plunge into a new career in tech, and am eager to continue learning and growing in the field.

My previous experiences in marketing and communications have given me unique perspective and insight on app development from creation to client-side. As a multi-passionate individual, I am always looking for new ways to expand my knowledge, and channel this in my day-to-day life as a developer. Nothing beats seeing projects come to life, from start to finish.
